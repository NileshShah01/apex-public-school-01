Academic Programs

Apex Public School offers comprehensive academic programs from Play Group to Class 8. Our curriculum is designed to provide strong foundational knowledge while preparing students for future competitive examinations.

Pre-Primary

Play Group, Nursery, LKG, UKG

Focus on early childhood development through play-based learning, motor skills development, and social interaction.

Primary

Class 1 to Class 5

Building strong foundations in core subjects including English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies.

Middle School

Class 6 to Class 8

Advanced learning with focus on analytical thinking, problem-solving, and preparation for board examinations.

Teaching Methodology

  • Interactive and engaging classroom sessions
  • Smart classroom technology for enhanced learning
  • Regular assessments and progress tracking
  • Practical learning through experiments and activities
  • Focus on conceptual understanding
  • Individual attention to each student
  • Co-curricular activities integration
  • Regular homework and assignments

Examination System

We follow a continuous and comprehensive evaluation system that includes:

  • Periodic tests and assessments
  • Half-yearly examinations
  • Annual examinations
  • Project work and assignments
  • Regular parent-teacher meetings
  • Progress reports and feedback

Competitive Exam Preparation

We prepare students for future competitive examinations through:

  • Strong foundation in core subjects
  • Logical and analytical thinking development
  • Problem-solving skills enhancement
  • Time management techniques
  • Mock tests and practice sessions
