Welcome to Apex Public School

Apex Public School, located in Anjani Bazar, Parsa, Saran, Bihar, is dedicated to providing quality education to students from Play Group to Class 8. Established with a vision to empower young minds, we have been serving the community with excellence and dedication.

Our school is recognized by the government with UDISE code 10171706503 and registration number 2111449. We take pride in offering a nurturing environment where every child can grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and quality education that prepares students for future challenges. We are committed to supporting students from all economic backgrounds and ensuring they receive the guidance needed to excel in competitive examinations and life.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in the region, known for academic excellence, character building, and holistic development. We envision creating responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.
