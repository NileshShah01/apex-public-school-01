Our Facilities

Modern infrastructure for quality education

Smart Classrooms

Our smart classrooms are equipped with modern digital technology including projectors and interactive displays. This enables teachers to deliver engaging lessons using multimedia content, making learning more interactive and effective. Students can visualize complex concepts and enjoy a dynamic learning experience.

Computer Lab

Well-equipped computer laboratory with latest systems and high-speed internet connectivity. Students receive hands-on training in computer applications, programming basics, and digital literacy. The lab is supervised by trained instructors to ensure proper guidance and safe internet usage.

Sports Ground

Spacious sports ground for various outdoor activities including cricket, football, athletics, and other games. We believe in holistic development and encourage students to participate in sports for physical fitness, teamwork, and discipline. Regular sports events and competitions are organized.

Playground with Equipment

Dedicated play area for younger students with swings, slides, see-saws, and other play equipment. The playground is designed keeping child safety in mind with soft landing surfaces. It provides a fun environment for children to develop motor skills and social interaction.

Library

Well-stocked library with a collection of books covering various subjects, story books, and reference materials. Students are encouraged to develop reading habits and explore knowledge beyond textbooks. The library provides a quiet environment for study and research.

Science Lab

Functional science laboratory for practical experiments and demonstrations. Students can perform experiments under supervision to understand scientific concepts better. The lab is equipped with essential apparatus and safety equipment.

Drinking Water

Clean and safe drinking water facility with water purifiers installed at multiple locations in the school premises. We ensure hygienic water supply for all students and staff throughout the day. Regular maintenance and testing of water quality is conducted.

CCTV Security

24/7 CCTV surveillance system covering all areas of the school campus for enhanced security. The safety of our students is our top priority. The security system is monitored continuously to ensure a safe learning environment.

School Transport

Safe and reliable transport facility for students from nearby areas. Our school buses are well-maintained and operated by experienced drivers. Each bus is accompanied by a staff member to ensure student safety during transit.

Clean Washrooms

Hygienic and well-maintained washroom facilities with separate arrangements for boys and girls. Regular cleaning and maintenance ensure cleanliness standards. Adequate water supply and hand-wash facilities are provided.

First Aid

Basic first aid facility available on campus for emergency medical situations. Trained staff can provide immediate assistance in case of minor injuries or health issues. Parents are informed immediately in case of any medical emergency.
